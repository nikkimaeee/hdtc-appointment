About Us

Ocampo Dental Clinic is a private dental facility established in 2010 and located at 1107 McArthur Hi-way Camachiles, Mabalacat City, Pampanga. Operated by Dr. Armina L. Ocampo D.M.D. at Ocampo Dental Clinic, our goal is to ensure every patient leaves with a healthy, confident smile, while fostering long-term relationships based on trust, respect, and quality care.

We strive to make dental care accessible, comfortable, and a positive experience for all.

  • Deliver High-Quality Care: Provide expert dental services with a focus on patient comfort and precision.
  • Enhance Oral Health Education: Empower patients with knowledge and tools for maintaining optimal oral hygiene.
  • Improve Patient Experience: Ensure every visit is efficient, welcoming, and anxiety-free.
  • Expand Access to Care: Offer affordable services and flexible payment options to reach more community members.
  • Gentleness & Kindness - We know that dental care can sometimes feel intimidating, so we make sure to greet every patient with warm smiles and a caring approach.
  • Patient Comfort Comes First- Your comfort is our priority! we believe in making sure every patient feels relaxed and at ease from the moment they walk in.
  • Transparency & Trust - We believe in being open and honest with our patients about their treatment plans. Building trust is our foundation.
  • Continuous Growth & Learning- Our team is constantly learning and growing. We value staying curious and continuously improving our skills to provide the best care possible.
  • Family-Friendly Care - we welcome patients of all ages with open arms and make sure everyone feels like they’re at home when visiting our clinic.
  • Community Love- We love our community! Our clinic is rooted in the belief that giving back and getting involved is just as important as providing care. We believe that when you’re happy, we’ve succeeded!

Meet Our Dentist

Dr. Armina L. Ocampo, DMD

Meet Dr. Armina L. Ocampo, D.M.D. Founder and Lead Dentist | Ocampo Dental Clinic Dr. Armina L. Ocampo, D.M.D., is a highly experienced dentist with over 10 years of practice in the field. She earned her degree in Dentistry from the Manila Central University in 2004 and has since been dedicated to providing exceptional dental care to the community of Mabalacat City, Pampanga. Her clinic, Ocampo Dental Clinic, established in 2010, offers a range of services including orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry, and emergency care.
